🌆 About Warsaw

Warsaw is Poland’s capital — a dynamic hub of culture, education, and innovation.

  • 📍 Location: Central Poland
  • 👥 Population: ~1.8 million
  • 💬 Language: Polish (English widely used in universities/services)
  • 💰 Currency: Polish złoty (PLN)
  • 🚨 Emergency: 112
  • 🚇 Transport: ZTM buses & metro, SKM trains, city bikes
